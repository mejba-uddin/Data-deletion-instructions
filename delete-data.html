Account Deletion & Privacy

At HeavensBD, we value your privacy and give you full control over your personal information. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Log in to your HeavensBD account.
  2. Go to your Profile page.
  3. Click on the “Delete Account” option.
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted.

Once confirmed, your account and all associated personal data will be permanently deleted from our system. This includes your profile information, order history, and any other stored data.

Important Notes

  • After deletion, your account cannot be recovered.
  • Certain minimal information may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., transaction records).
  • If you face any issues deleting your account, please contact our support team at support@heavensbd.com
