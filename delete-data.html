Account Deletion & Data Privacy

At HeavensBD, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. This page explains how you can delete your account and request data removal.

You can request deletion of your account or data anytime by following the instructions below.

🗑️ How to Delete Your Account

  1. Log in to your HeavensBD account
  2. Go to Profile Settings
  3. Click Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

After deletion, all your personal data will be permanently removed from our system.

📌 Data We May Store

  • Facebook public profile (name, profile picture)
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Usage activity within our app

📨 Request Data Deletion (Facebook Users)

  • Email: support@heavensbd.com
  • Subject: Facebook Data Deletion Request
  • Include your User ID or email
Request Deletion

⏱️ Processing Time

We process all requests within 7 business days.

🔐 Data Security

Your data is securely handled and permanently deleted after verification.
